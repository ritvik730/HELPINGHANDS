About Us

We are HelpingHands, a small but motivated online store providing the platform to the students of the college to sell their hostel or college product like books, notes, curtain, tables, cooler etc. so that the new coming student don't have to worry about to buy new product just for 2-3 months.

Our aim to continue provide a great platform so that a new comely student can find all their required product so they need not have to worry about the near shop and also about the cost of ech product. Because here they can get the all products in half of the price so they can reuse the product and save their money.
